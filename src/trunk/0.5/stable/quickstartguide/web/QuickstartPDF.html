PDF Presentation Viewer Quickstart Guide
The PDF Presentation Viewer is a multi-user tool for sharing presentations and other documents in PDF Format.
Find a PDF presentation and drag and drop it into an open Wonderland window.
The document will show up in front of your avatar. Note that if you have another application installed that reads PDF, such as the PDF Spreader, you will be asked to choose which application to use.
To control the slides, right click on the presentation and select "Take Control."
The window border will turn from red to green, a tool palette will open, and a "Release App Control" button will appear in the upper right of your Wonderland window.
To go to the next slide, click on the right arrow tool.
Everyone in the world will see the slides advance. If you are not the presenter, you should not click on the set of tools that change the slides without first unsynchronizing your view, as explained in Step 4.
If you are not the presenter, you may also take control of the slides. To browse on your own, for example, to go back and review a previous slide, click on the gear icon to unsynchronize your view.
The gears will open. When you want to return to the slide that the presenter is currently discussing, click on the same tool to re-synchronize your view. The gears will be in the closed position when you are sychronized with the presenter.

Tips
Presentation Viewer Uses
Use the PDF Presentation Viewer to share talk slides, documentation, or any other content that does not need to modified.
Watching and Walking
Right-click and select "Show in HUD" to watch the presentation while you move your avatar around the world.
Slides Not Changing?
If the presenter sounds like they're on a slide that you're not seeing, be sure your view is synchronized.
Can't Move?
Don't forgot to click "Release App Control."
If that doesn't unstick you, try selecting "Avatar Collision Response Enabled" from the Tools menu to turn off collision.
