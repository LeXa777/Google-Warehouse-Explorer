Whiteboard Quickstart Guide
The Whiteboard is a multi-user tool for creating simple drawings and diagrams together.
Select "Object" from the Insert menu. Find and select "Whiteboard (SVG)" in the Insert Object dialog and then click on the "Insert" button.
To use the Whiteboard, right click on the window and select "Take Control."
The window border will turn from red to green, a tool palette will open, and a "Release App Control" button will appear in the upper right of your Wonderland window.
Click on one of the drawing tools and select a color.
Drag out a line, rectangle, or oval on the Whiteboard, or click in a location to add text.
By default, the rectangles and ovals will be unfilled. To created filled shapes, click on the tool to the right of the "T".
And then click on a color. Now when you draw, the shapes will be filled.
Multiple users can take control of the whiteboard at the same time and draw or rearrange objects.
When done, click the "Release App Control" button in the upper right corner of the Wonderland window.

Tips
Whiteboard Uses
Use the Whiteboard to sketch out ideas, take notes, or create simple diagrams.
Watching and Walking
Right-click and select "Show in HUD" to watch others work on the Whiteboard while you move your avatar around the world.
Point
Select "Telepointer" from the View menu to point to things on the Whiteboard.
